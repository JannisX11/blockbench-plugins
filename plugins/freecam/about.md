Freecam adds a fly camera mode for the viewport. Unlike orbit navigation, the mouse turns the camera directly and WASD moves relative to where you are looking.

Enable it from the View menu or press F6. Click the viewport to capture the mouse, then fly with WASD. Press R and F to move up and down. Hold Shift to move faster, Alt to move slower. Press Escape to release the mouse.

The speed and mouse sensitivity can be changed in the settings under Preview. Movement keys can be remapped in the keybindings menu.

Do not use this plugin together with the WASD Controls plugin.
